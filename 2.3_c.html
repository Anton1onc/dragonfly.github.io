Dragonfly kernel v.2.3 Stable | Redmi 7

by @DfP_DEV | 8 Febraury 2020


  • Upstreamed to 4.9.213. Including related CAF Tags and Android (Q) support Patches
  • Improved work with a small amount of RAM
  • A strong addition to responsiveness and some to smoothness
  • Adaptation. Now the kernel will work no less smoothly even if the tweaks/profiles you haven't earned correctly,
    or aren't installed. Less lag when charging below 10%
  • Less lag when charging below 10%
  • New kernel manager called DfK (short for kernel name)
  • Smart mode - automatic adjustment of the profile to the situation.
    You can enable and configure from the DfK application.
  • The smartphone will warm up much less when charging.
    It is also possible to increase the charge rate as such. It works when smart mode is turned on.
  • Added random number generator FRandom, to replace the standard one.
